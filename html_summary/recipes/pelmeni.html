★ Pelmeni Making Adventure ★

(◕‿◕✿) ~ Let’s make fluffy little Russian dumplings together!



Russian pelmeni are not just food — they are tiny fluffy ears filled with a secret! Imagine the softest dough, like a little cloud on a sunny day, hiding a juicy meat surprise inside. When you take your first bite, it goes PUWAAAA and sends warm happiness all through your body (๑˃̵ᴗ˂̵)و They usually dance around in boiling water, swirling like little snowballs, and then get served with golden butter, rosy sour cream, or even a splash of tangy vinegar — whatever your heart desires! But did you know... here’s a real Siberian secret: back in the old days, whole families would gather to make hundreds of pelmeni, then take them outside in bags during freezing winter — and they would stay perfectly preserved for months, like little meat treasures 🧊 And in some villages, people used to hide a surprise inside one or two pelmeni — a peppercorn or a tiny piece of ginger for good luck ✨ (if you find it — happiness is coming your way!) Homemade pelmeni are like a warm hug from your grandmother in a bowl: simple, cozy, and so comforting that even a tsundere would crack a smile (//>/ ▽ /<//)

★ INGREDIENTS ★

(｡•̀ᴗ-)✧

For the dough:

  • 2 cups flour (plus extra for punching? Nope, for dusting! hehe~)
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup water (WAUWWW so simple!!)
  • 1 tbsp oil (shiny shiny ✨)
  • Pinch of salt

For the filling: (WAAAA SO GOOD!!)

  • 300g ground pork + beef (meat powerrr ⚡)
  • 1 small onion (minced finely〜 crying kawaii tears)
  • Salt, black pepper, iced water (2 tbsp)

For the sauce:

    Sour cream or butter + vinegar + soy sauce (UMAI!!)

★ LET'S COOK LIKE A PROTAGONIST ★

(•̀ᴗ•́)و Anime determination eyes

  1. Make the dough (｡◕‿◕｡)

    In a big bowl, mix flour + salt. Make a tiny volcano crater ⛰️, crack the egg inside, add oil + water. Mix with chopsticks (or hands hehe). Knead until smooth and soft like mochi skin. Sugēēē!! (ﾉ◕ヮ◕)ﾉ

    Let it rest under a wet towel for 30 mins — it’s taking a cute nap! zzz (－‿－✿)

  2. Make the filling (ᗒᗨᗕ)

    Mix minced meat + onion + salt + pepper in a bowl. Add iced water and mix with chopsticks until sticky and shiny. WOOOOW it smells like victory (๑˃ᴗ˂)ﻭ

  3. Roll & cut dough ☆

    Roll dough super thin (like anime character’s patience hehe). Cut circles with a cup (5 cm wide). WAAAAA PERFECT CIRCLES!

  4. Fold into cute pelmeni (✿◠‿◠)

    Put a small ball of meat in the center. Fold into half-moon, pinch edges tightly. Then connect the two corners — now it looks like a tiny adorable ear! KYAAA SO CUTE!! (//>/ ▽ /<//)

  5. Boil with sparkles! ✨

    Drop pelmeni into boiling salted water. Stir gently (don't hurt them!). When they float — wait 3–5 mins more. VAVVV THEY ARE DANCING! ♪♪♪

  6. Serving time ♡

    Put on a plate, add melted butter or sour cream. Sprinkle with dill or black pepper. Say: “Itadakimasuuuu!!” (≧◡≦) ♡

★ FINAL BITE ★

When you eat… the juice goes PUWAAAA in your mouth!! UMAMI EXPLOSION! ⸜(｡> ᵕ < )⸝♡
