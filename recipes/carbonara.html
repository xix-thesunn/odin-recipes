★ Carbonara Delizioso ★

(◕‿◕✿) – Italian magic in an anime kitchen!

★ INGREDIENTS ★

(｡•̀ᴗ-)✧ FOR 2 HUNGRY PROTAGONISTS

The Pasta (〜￣▽￣)〜:

  • 200g spaghetti (or any long pasta — WAAA SO ELEGANT!)
  • Salt for pasta water (like a little ocean breeze 🌊)

The Creamy Sauce (no cream! real Roman magic ☆)

  • 2 large eggs (fresh and shiny ✨)
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese (grated — BAAA SO SALTY GOOD!!)
  • 20g Parmigiano (extra umami power ⚡)
  • Black pepper (freshly cracked — CRACKLE CRACKLE 🌟)

The Meaty Star (ᗒᗨᗕ)

  • 100g guanciale (or pancetta/bacon — CRISPY PIGGY WIGGY 🐷)
  • 1 clove garlic (optional but WOOOOW SO AROMATIC!!)

★ LET’S COOK LIKE A CHEF IN AN ANIME OPENING ★

(•̀ᴗ•́)و Spinning pasta fork, determined eyes

  1. Boil the pasta (VAVVV MOST IMPORTANT!!)

    Fill a big pot with water. Add LOTS of salt (like the sea — not the Dead Sea, but close hehe).

    Wait until it’s rolly polly boiling — WAAAAA BUBBLES EVERYWHERE!!

    Add spaghetti. Cook until al dente (a little bite — GANBATTE, PASTA!!).

    Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining — DON’T FORGET!! Sugēēē!! ✨

  2. Crisp the meat (KYAAA SO NOISY AND GOOD!!)

    Cut guanciale into small cubes (or cute little strips ♡). Put in a cold pan — then turn on medium heat.

    Let the fat melt slowly… sizzle sizzle… until crispy and GOLDEN.

    WOOOOW IT SMELLS LIKE HEAVEN!! (๑>ᴗ<)

    Turn off heat. (If using garlic, add it gently and remove — just for a kiss of flavour 💋)

  3. Make the sauce in a bowl (magical egg mixture ☆)

    In a separate bowl, crack 2 eggs. Whisk like you’re casting a spell — furiously but gently ♪

    Add both grated cheeses + lots of black pepper. Mix mix mix.

    WAAAAA IT BECOMES A YELLOW CLOUD!! ⸜(｡> ᵕ < )⸝

  4. The EMULSION — most dramatic part ★

    Put the drained hot pasta directly into the pan with crispy guanciale (heat is OFF!).

    Toss to coat every strand in porky fat — KYAAA SHINY NOODLES!!

    Let it cool for 30 seconds (IMPORTANT — or eggs will scramble = sad anime ending).

    Pour the egg+cheese mixture over pasta. Add a splash of reserved pasta water.

    Toss toss toss like a magical girl transformation — WAAAAA IT BECOMES CREAMY WITHOUT CREAM!!

    Science and love together! (//>/ ▽ /<//)

  5. Adjust and serve (◕‿◕✿)

    Add more pasta water if too thick. Add more pepper if you’re brave.

    Twirl pasta into a cute nest on a warm plate.

    Grate extra cheese on top — make it snow!! ❄️✨

    Sprinkle black pepper like little stars 🌟

  6. The first bite ~

    Say “ITALIAN ITADAKIMASUUUU!!” with sparkly eyes ✨

    Forks up — twiiiiirl — into the mouth — PUWAAAA CREAMY, SALTY, PEPPERY EXPLOSION!! (≧◡≦) ♡


★ ANIME TIPS FOR PERFECT CARBONARA ★

(◕ᴗ◕✿)

  • No cream! Real Carbonara never uses cream — that’s a lie of the food devils! 😈
  • Hot pasta, cold pan, warm eggs = emulsification magic ☆
  • Reserve pasta water — it’s literally liquid gold WAAAAA
  • Eat immediately — Carbonara waits for no one, not even the final episode cliffhanger!
