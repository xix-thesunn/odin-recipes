★ Sushi For Beginners ★

(◕‿◕✿) – Make sushi without crying! (well, maybe happy tears~)

★ INGREDIENTS ★

(｡•̀ᴗ-)✧ SUPER SIMPLE MODE

For the rice (the heart of sushi ♡):

  • 1 cup sushi rice (short grain — WAAA SO STICKY!)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt

For the filling (KYAAA CHOOSE YOUR FAVES ⸜(｡> ᵕ < )⸝):

  • Cucumber (cut into sticks — CRISPY BOIS 🥒)
  • Avocado (soft and buttery — KIMI NO NA WA… AVOCADO!! 🥑)
  • Crab sticks (imitation crab — TOTSUGEKI!!)
  • Or tuna can (drain first — MAGURO POWAA!! 🐟)

For the wrapper + tools:

  • Nori (seaweed sheets — CRUNCHY BLACK PAPER 🌊)
  • Bamboo mat (makisu — your sushi bestie hehe)
  • Water bowl (for wet hands — SPLISH SPLASH 💦)

For dipping:

  • Soy sauce (shoyu — THE DARK POTION 🥢)
  • Wasabi (green spicy cloud — GANBATTE!!)
  • Pickled ginger (pink and sweet — KAWAIIIIII ♡)

★ LET'S COOK LIKE A PROTAGONIST ★

(•̀ᴗ•́)و Anime determination eyes

  1. Cook the rice (WAAAAA MOST IMPORTANT!!)

    Rinse rice until water is clear — like your soul after anime marathon (˘⌣˘) Cook with 1 cup water (rice cooker or pot). After cooking, let it rest 10 mins. CLAAAASS! ✨

  2. Make sushi vinegar (magic potion time ☆)

    Mix rice vinegar + sugar + salt. Microwave 20 sec (WOOOOW IT SMELLS LIKE SUSHI RESTAURANT!!)

    Pour over hot rice. Mix with wooden spoon — cutting motion (not smashing! be gentle like senpai ♡)

    Fan the rice while mixing — it becomes SHINY SHINY!! (๑˃ᴗ˂)ﻭ

  3. Prepare your fillings (◕‿◕✿)

    Cut cucumber, avocado, crab sticks into long thin strips.

    VAVVV SO COLOURFUL!! Arrange on plate like a magical girl transformation sequence.

  4. Roll the maki sushi (KYAAA beginner friendly!!)

    1. Put nori on bamboo mat (rough side up — SHINY SIDE DOWN).
    2. Wet your hands (so rice won’t stick — ehehe~).
    3. Spread thin layer of rice on nori (leave 1 cm at top edge free).
    4. Lay fillings in a line (don’t overstuff — GAMBARIMASU!).
    5. Roll using mat — squeeze gently but firmly. WAUUUU IT’S TURNING INTO SUSHI!!
    6. Wet the top edge of nori to seal.

  5. Cut into cute pieces ☆

    Wet knife (very important!). Cut roll into 6–8 pieces.

    KYAAAAAAAA PERFECT CIRCLES WITH RAINBOW INSIDE! (//>/ ▽ /<//)

  6. Serve with drama ~

    Arrange on plate. Add wasabi (tiny green mountain 🏔️), ginger (pink flower 🌸), soy sauce in small bowl.

    Say “ITADAKIMASUUUU!!” with sparkly eyes ✨

★ EXTRA: ONIGIRI MODE (for scared beginners ♡) ★

Too scared to roll? Just make rice balls!

Wet hands + salt → make triangle rice → wrap with small nori strip → add filling inside (like secret treasure 🎁).

WAAAAA EVEN EASIER! ⸜(｡> ᵕ < )⸝

★ FINAL BITE ★

When you dip in soy sauce… PUWAAAA umami explosion!!
Wasabi goes TSUN☆ in your nose — but in a good way!! (≧◡≦) ♡
